Why I Chose This Theme

You may be wondering why I chose a nature theme for a tech blog. While I love learning about new technologies, I also enjoy occassionally getting away from computers and phones to recharge myself and the best way to do that for me is to get outside. When I can (i.e. when the weather is cooperating) I even try to work outside and find I'm better able to focus.


Maybe you aren't reading this blog outside, so I thought I would bring the outdoors to the reader to emanate a sense of calm and induce focus. I didn't want something that hit the reader right in the face, since we get enough of that throughout our daily lives and at the end of the day is overwhelming.


So please enjoy as I talk about some tech that I'm excited about.
