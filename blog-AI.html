You Can Become a Novelist...Sort Of...

If you're like me, writing may just not come naturally to you, especially when it comes to creative writing. So when I discovered there were AI's out there that could write a novel, I was intrigued. I tried out NovelAI


At NovelAI, all you have to do is enter a prompt and it will start writing a story based on that prompt - pretty cool! Though the story sometimes made no sense, many sentences were repetitive and it never really had a plot or a story arc, but it helped me get some creative juices flowing. Sometimes I would start with a prompt, but then turned into an ad for a game. It even comes up with descriptions and tag lines./p>
